Aged people must be well looked after once they cross the prime years of their lives. Institutions can offer free lodging and meals to those in need of it. Natural Resources

All elders and aged people must be kept occupied by keeping themselves busy via a form of occupation, they can be given work in non- government organization, libraries, schools and offices. Jobs for Widowed Women

In India, widows are looked down upon as disgraceful, unlucky – even cursed. When a couple marries the woman “divorces” her family and lives with her husband family. Once the husband dies, relatives take away all of the property, sell it and divide the profits. They often leave the wife and children with nothing, they are left on their own, living sad lives of destitution and poverty. Indian villages thus girls get married at a very tender age.

About us

Umeed is a non-profit making, voluntary, development oriented organization that has been established 1997 to fight against various social injustices by providing education and health care, organising awareness camps against violence, dowry, child marriages, girls abortion and environmental pollution and by supporting women in their empowerment.
