ANIMAL WELFARE

As per the India tradition and culture, animal always had respect and special place in society.

But today time have changed.

With population explosion, urbanization and consumerism catching up, animal are easy prey for human greed.

They are soft targets for anyone trying to make a quick buck, and prime candidates for exploitation.

With so many animal issues and so many animals on the streets, the need of the day is to have several animal welfare societies in each community.

The only ray of hope is the advent of television.

Awareness about animal issues and environment is spreading with youngsters watching channels like animal planet, national geographic and discovery.

They are becoming aware of the place of animals in our lives and the need to give them due respect.

About us

Umeed is a non-profit making, voluntary, development oriented organization that has been established 1997 to fight against various social injustices by providing education and health care, organising awareness camps against violence, dowry, child marriages, girls abortion and environmental pollution and by supporting women in their empowerment.
